Tomodachi

Made with the Raspberry Pi

Tomodachi is a portable application to test one's knowledge of the kana. I made it so that I could test myself on the Kana on my way to work. It runs off a Raspberry Pi 3 coupled with a WaveShare e-ink screen. #python2 raspberry-pi
